Interested with how we work?

At the start of every project, we work closely with the client, gathering the base features of their site, including the style and the layout.

Combining all this infomation, the team then goes away to produce an MVP (Minimum Viviable Product.) This allows to go back to the client and get their thoughts on the design and structure of the site.

After the tweaks and changes are done, and the client is happy with the design the website is then tested on various different devices and in different browsers. This ensure that no matter what device the clients customer is using, the site will still operate as normal.

The final stage is production, wether hosted by us or another company the site is then made visable on the chosen domain of the client!
